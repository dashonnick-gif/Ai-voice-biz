# VoxLocal Customer Onboarding Guide

Welcome to VoxLocal! We're excited to help you stop missing calls and start growing your business. Follow these steps to get your 24/7 AI Receptionist up and running.

---

## Step 1: Tell Us About Your Business
To provide the best service, your AI assistant needs to know the basics. Please fill out your **Business Profile** in the VoxLocal dashboard:
*   **Business Hours**: Regular and holiday hours.
*   **Services & Pricing**: A list of what you offer so the AI can answer basic questions.
*   **FAQs**: Common questions like "Where do I park?" or "What insurance do you take?"

## Step 2: Connect Your Calendar
VoxLocal can book appointments directly for you.
1.  Go to **Integrations** in the dashboard.
2.  Select your provider (Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Calendly, etc.).
3.  Authorize VoxLocal to read availability and create events.
4.  Define your **Appointment Types** (e.g., "Dental Cleaning - 60 mins").

## Step 3: Customize Your AI Assistant
Make the assistant feel like part of your team.
*   **Choose a Voice**: Select from our range of professional and friendly voices.
*   **Set the Greeting**: e.g., "Welcome to BrightSmile Dental Clinic, how can I help you today?"
*   **Knowledge Base**: Upload any specific documents or lists the AI should know.

## Step 4: Activate Your Line
There are two ways to use VoxLocal:
1.  **Direct Number**: Use the dedicated local number we've provided you on your website and marketing.
2.  **Call Forwarding**: Set up your existing office line to "Forward on Busy" or "Forward on No Answer" to your VoxLocal number. This ensures no call goes unanswered even when your team is on the other line.

## Step 5: Monitor & Refine
*   **Dashboard**: Log in to see call logs, listen to recordings, and see appointments booked.
*   **Feedback**: You can "Correct" the AI on any call. This helps it learn and improve for next time.
