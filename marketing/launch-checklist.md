# VoxLocal Landing Page Launch Checklist

This checklist outlines the necessary content and technical steps to prepare the VoxLocal landing page for public launch.

## 1. SEO & Social Meta Tags
- [ ] **Meta Title**: VoxLocal | 24/7 AI Voice Receptionist for Local Businesses
- [ ] **Meta Description**: Never miss a lead again. VoxLocal provides AI-powered voice agents that answer calls, book appointments, and handle FAQs for dental clinics, salons, and local services.
- [ ] **OG Image**: Design a 1200x630px preview image showing the VoxLocal interface and value proposition.
- [ ] **Favicon**: Create a high-resolution favicon based on the VoxLocal phone/gradient logo.

## 2. Page Content & Social Proof
- [ ] **Headline Verification**: Ensure the hero headline aligns with the latest business plan (Focus on "Never Miss a Lead").
- [ ] **Testimonials (Placeholders)**:
    - *Dental Clinic*: "VoxLocal handled our Saturday emergency calls perfectly. We booked 5 new patients while the office was closed." - Dr. Aris Smith (Placeholder)
    - *Salon Owner*: "Our front desk is much calmer now that the AI handles all the routine booking questions." - Sarah J. (Placeholder)
- [ ] **Vertical-Specific Copy**: Ensure dental-specific examples are balanced with general service business language.

## 3. Legal & Compliance
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: Link to a standard privacy policy covering lead data collection (Name, Phone Number).
- [ ] **Terms of Service**: Link to terms of service regarding AI-handled communications and call recording disclosures.
- [ ] **Cookie Banner**: Implement if using tracking scripts (Google Analytics, etc.).

## 4. Technical Tracking & Analytics
- [ ] **Google Analytics (G4)**: Install tracking code to monitor visitor behavior and traffic sources.
- [ ] **Facebook Pixel**: (Optional) Install if running social media ads.
- [ ] **Hotjar/Clarity**: (Optional) For heatmaps to optimize conversion paths.

## 5. Conversion Path & CTA
- [ ] **CTA Destination**: Confirm where "Start Free Trial" and "Get Started" buttons lead (e.g., a Stripe checkout or a Typeform lead capture).
- [ ] **Form Success Page**: Create a clear "Thank You" page with next steps (e.g., "Check your inbox for onboarding instructions").
- [ ] **Email Automation**: Connect lead forms to an automated "Welcome" email sequence.

## 6. Final Polishing
- [ ] **Mobile Responsiveness**: Test on iPhone and Android devices.
- [ ] **Page Speed**: Optimize image sizes and minimize JS load times.
- [ ] **Broken Links**: Verify all navigation links and social icons work correctly.
