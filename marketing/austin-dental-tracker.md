## Status Definitions
- **Prospecting**: Initial research done, ready for first contact.
- **Contacted**: First email or phone call completed.
- **Demo Booked**: Customer agreed to a live demo of the voice agent.
- **Trial Started**: Customer is currently using the voice agent in a test capacity.
- **Closed Won**: Customer signed a monthly subscription.
- **Closed Lost**: Customer declined or is no longer interested.
- **Stale Data**: Website dead or business details unverifiable. Needs phone verification before outreach.
- **REMOVE**: Invalid for this market (wrong location, out of business, etc.).
