# VoxLocal Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: June 24, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

VoxLocal ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered voice agent services for local businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, services, and platform (collectively, the "Services").

By using VoxLocal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Business name, contact name, email address, phone number, billing address, and payment details when you create an account or subscribe to a plan.
- **Business Profile Data**: Business hours, services offered, pricing, FAQ responses, calendar integration credentials, and custom scripts or voice preferences.
- **Customer Support Communications**: Emails, chat messages, or phone calls with our support team.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Call Data**: Audio recordings of calls handled by our AI voice agents, transcripts generated from those calls, caller phone numbers, call duration, timestamps, and call outcomes (e.g., appointment booked, FAQ answered).
- **Usage Data**: Log data, IP addresses, browser type, device information, pages visited, and interactions with our dashboard.
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve our Services. See our Cookie Policy for details.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Calendar Providers**: With your authorization, we access calendar availability from Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Calendly, and similar services.
- **Payment Processors**: We receive limited transaction data from Stripe to process payments.
- **Telephony Providers**: We receive caller ID and routing information from telephony partners (e.g., Twilio) to connect calls to our AI agents.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Provide and Operate the Services**: Answer calls, book appointments, respond to FAQs, and deliver call summaries.
- **Improve Our AI**: Train and refine our voice recognition, natural language understanding, and conversation models using anonymized call transcripts.
- **Billing and Administration**: Process payments, manage subscriptions, send invoices, and handle account-related communications.
- **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance.
- **Security and Compliance**: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with legal obligations.
- **Communications**: Send service updates, feature announcements, and marketing emails (you can opt out of marketing at any time).

---

## 4. Call Recordings and Transcripts

### 4.1 Recording of Calls
VoxLocal records and transcribes phone calls handled by our AI agents. **You are responsible for complying with all applicable call-recording laws**, including obtaining consent from callers where required by law (e.g., two-party consent states).

### 4.2 How We Use Recordings
- We store call recordings and transcripts to provide call logs, quality assurance, and AI improvement.
- We may review a small sample of calls for quality assurance and training purposes.
- We do not sell call recordings or transcripts to third parties.

### 4.3 Retention and Deletion
- Call recordings are retained for **90 days** by default, unless you choose a longer retention period in your dashboard.
- You can delete individual call recordings or transcripts at any time from your dashboard.
- Upon account cancellation, all call data is permanently deleted within **30 days**, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted vendors who help us operate the Services (e.g., cloud hosting, telephony, payment processing, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
- **Legal Compliance**: When required by law, subpoena, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality obligations.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- Role-based access controls for our internal systems.
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (targeted for Q3 2026).

No system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@voxlocal.com.

---

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Portability**: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing activities, such as marketing or AI training using your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@voxlocal.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy

VoxLocal is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us to have it removed.
