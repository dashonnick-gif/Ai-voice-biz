## 4. Key Features
### ⚡ Instant Answer, Zero Hold
Your customers get an immediate response every single time. No "please hold," no waiting in queues. Just instant service.

### 📅 Smart Appointment Booking
VoxLocal syncs directly with your existing calendar (Google, Outlook, Calendly, and more). It checks your availability and books the spot — no manual entry required.

### 🧠 Deep Business Knowledge
Train your agent on your specific FAQs. From "What are your holiday hours?" to "Do you handle specialized dental implants?", VoxLocal has the answers.

### 📱 Lead Qualification & CRM Sync
VoxLocal doesn't just take messages; it asks the right questions. It gathers name, contact info, and service needs, then sends them straight to your CRM or inbox.
