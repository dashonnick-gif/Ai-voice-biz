# VoxLocal Demo Script: BrightSmile Dental Clinic

**Scenario**: A potential patient, Sarah, calls BrightSmile Dental Clinic on a Saturday afternoon when the clinic is closed. VoxLocal, the AI receptionist, answers the call.

---

### **The Conversation Flow**

**[Phone Ringing]**

**VoxLocal**: "Thank you for calling BrightSmile Dental Clinic. This is your AI assistant. How can I help you today?"

**Sarah**: "Hi, I'd like to book an appointment for a cleaning. I have a bit of a toothache too."

**VoxLocal**: "I'm sorry to hear about your toothache, Sarah. We can certainly get you scheduled for a cleaning and have the doctor take a look at that tooth. Are you a new or returning patient at BrightSmile Dental Clinic?"

**Sarah**: "I'm a new patient."

**VoxLocal**: "Welcome! We’d love to have you. To get started, could you please tell me your full name and a good phone number to reach you at?"

**Sarah**: "My name is Sarah Miller, and my number is 555-0123."

**VoxLocal**: "Got it, Sarah. Thank you. Now, let's look at the schedule. Dr. Aris has openings this coming Tuesday. We have slots at 10:00 AM, 1:30 PM, and 4:00 PM. Do any of those work for you?"

**Sarah**: "Tuesday at 1:30 PM sounds perfect."

**VoxLocal**: "Excellent choice. Just to confirm, I have you down for a cleaning and a toothache exam with Dr. Aris this Tuesday at 1:30 PM. Does that all look correct?"

**Sarah**: "Yes, that's right."

**VoxLocal**: "Perfect. I’ve reserved that spot for you. You’ll receive a confirmation text shortly with our address and a link to fill out your new patient forms online to save time. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

**Sarah**: "No, that's all. Thank you so much!"

**VoxLocal**: "You're very welcome, Sarah! We look forward to seeing you on Tuesday. Have a great day!"

**[Call Ends]**

---

### **Behind the Scenes: VoxLocal Actions**
1.  **Instant Greeting**: Answers on the first ring, even on weekends.
2.  **Intent Recognition**: Identifies "cleaning" and "toothache" as the primary reasons for the call.
3.  **Lead Capture**: Collects name and phone number for the new patient record.
4.  **Live Calendar Integration**: Checks real-time availability and presents options.
5.  **Direct Booking**: Places the appointment directly into the clinic's management software.
6.  **SMS Automation**: Triggers a confirmation text and digital intake forms.
