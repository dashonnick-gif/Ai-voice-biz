# VoxLocal Batch 1 Outreach Plan: Austin Dental Clinics

**Objective**: Convert high-volume Austin dental practices into free trial users or demo bookings.

---

## 1. Strategy & Target
- **Primary Vertical**: Dental Clinics.
- **Why**: High missed call rates after-hours and during lunch; high value per appointment.
- **Target List**: [Austin Prospect List](./prospect-list.md#1-dental-clinics) (12 clinics).

---

## 2. Outreach Cadence
We will use a multi-touch approach over 14 days to maximize response rates.

| Day | Action | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Day 1** | **Email 1** (Problem Hook) | Agitate the "missed call" pain point. |
| **Day 3** | **Phone Call 1** | Confirm they received the email; offer a "quick listen" to the AI. |
| **Day 5** | **Email 2** (Social Proof) | Show results from other clinics (BrightSmile Dental story). |
| **Day 8** | **Email 3** (The Offer) | Hard offer: 7-day free trial. |
| **Day 10** | **Phone Call 2** | Direct follow-up on the trial offer; handle setup objections. |
| **Day 14** | **Email 4** (Break-up) | Close the loop; maintain professional relationship. |

---

## 3. Phone Follow-up Scripts

### Script 1: Initial Follow-up (Day 3)
*Goal: Just get them to acknowledge the pain point and offer a quick demo link.*

> "Hi, is this the office manager? My name is [Your Name] from VoxLocal. I sent an email over on Monday about missed patient calls after hours. I'm not looking to sell you anything today, I just wanted to see if you're the right person to talk to about how you handle calls when the front desk is busy?"
>
> *(If yes)*: "Great. We've seen clinics in Austin lose about 60% of their after-hours leads to voicemail. I’d love to send you a 1-minute audio clip of our AI answering a dental call so you can hear how natural it sounds. Can I text that to this number?"

### Script 2: The Offer Follow-up (Day 10)
*Goal: Overcome setup friction and start a trial.*

> "Hi [Name], [Your Name] from VoxLocal again. I sent over an offer for a free 7-day trial of our AI receptionist. I know the biggest concern is usually 'how hard is this to set up?'
>
> It actually takes less than 10 minutes to sync with your calendar. If I sent you a setup link, would you be open to letting the AI handle your overflow calls for just one weekend to see the results?"

---

## 5. Vertical Adaptation Instructions
To roll this plan out to **Auto Repair** or **Salons**:
1. **Swap the Scripts**: Replace "patients" with "customers" or "clients".
2. **Update the Pain Point**: For HVAC/Auto, focus on "Emergency Service" calls. For Salons, focus on "Last-minute booking/rescheduling".
3. **Change the Social Proof**: Use a vertical-specific story (e.g., "The local shop that booked 10 oil changes while closed").
